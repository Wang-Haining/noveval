AutoPeer

Automatically Evaluating Scientific Novelty in Alignment with Human Assessments

AutoPeer has been proven capable of assessing scientific papers in a manner that significantly correlates with human experts (see our paper). Please paste the paper whose novelty you'd like to examine into the box. Note that the text should be longer than 2,560 tokens (~2,000 words).

The perplexity score for a sequence of 2,048 tokens is calculated and visualized. The deeper the shade, the greater the "surprise" a GPT-2 trained on the Wikipedia (English) corpus would experience. Hover over the tokens to view the surprisal value for each token.

AutoPeer serves as a useful tool for gaining insights into the novelty of scientific discourse. However, like all tools, it's essential to use it judiciously, taking into account its strengths and limitations.
